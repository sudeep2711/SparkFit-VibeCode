# SparkFit — Design System: Kinetic Pulse

## 1. Design Vision
SparkFit's "Kinetic Pulse" aesthetic is high-energy, modern, and tech-forward. It's designed to motivate users through a dark, immersive environment punctuated by vibrant neon accents and rhythmic textures. The interface feels alive, reactive, and professional, like a high-end gym's digital infrastructure.

---

## 2. Visual Language

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background:** `#131313` (Deep Obsidian) - Solid base for all screens.
- **Secondary Surface:** `#1c1b1b` (Tonal Shadow) - Used for layering and card depth.
- **Accent Neon:** `#2E7BFF` (Electric Blue) - Used for glows, highlights, and primary interactions.
- **Support Neon:** `#eaffb9` (Volt Lime) - High-contrast indicator for active states and goals.
- **Text Primary:** `#e5e2e1` (Off-White) - Crisp readability.
- **Text Secondary:** `#e5e2e1/60` (Muted Silver) - Hierarchy and metadata.

### Typography
- **Headlines:** `Space Grotesk` (Bold/Black) - Technical, geometric, and aggressive.
- **Body:** `Be Vietnam Pro` (Medium/Regular) - Clean, modern, and highly legible for stats and tips.

### Textures & Patterns
- **Halftone Signature:** A neon blue halftone dot pattern is used as a primary background texture for high-value cards (Total Workouts, Workout Plan highlights). It should "pop" with a subtle glow but remain behind content to maintain legibility.
- **Glows & Gradients:** Radial neon blue glows highlight active sessions and key buttons.

---

## 3. UI Components

### Cards & Sections
- **Interactive Cards:** Deep dark surfaces with rounded corners (`rounded-3xl`).
- **Data Visualization:** Thick, high-contrast progress bars using the Volt Lime (`#eaffb9`) and Electric Blue (`#2E7BFF`) accents.
- **Exercise List Items:** Clean, horizontal rows with clear icons and "forward" chevrons for navigation.

### Navigation
- **Top Bar:** Center-aligned branding with matted transparency or solid deep dark backgrounds.
- **Bottom Navigation:** Pill-shaped active indicators with high-contrast text and blurred background shells (`backdrop-blur-xl`).

### Specialized Elements
- **Workout Timer:** Large, italicized, or glowing digital-style typography to create a sense of urgency and focus.
- **AI Coach Tooltip:** Dedicated tonal blocks with a specialized "Spark" icon to differentiate AI advice from standard logging.
- **Exercise Media:** Integrated video/GIF players with rounded corners and consistent neon blue borders.

---

## 4. Interaction Principles
- **Clarity over Clutter:** Remove redundant labels and background noise (e.g., background numbers on cards) to keep the user focused on the immediate task.
- **Energetic State Changes:** Use neon color shifts and subtle scaling to indicate selection or completion.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Essential stats (Reps, Sets, Weight) should be the largest elements on workout execution screens.
